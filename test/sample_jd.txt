Senior Data Scientist – Personalization & Recommendations

Location: Seattle, WA (Hybrid)
Company: TechNova Inc.

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our Personalization team. You will design and deploy machine learning models that power real-time product recommendations for 50M+ users. You will work closely with engineering, product, and business teams to translate complex data into actionable insights and scalable ML solutions.

Responsibilities:
- Build and deploy end-to-end ML pipelines for recommendation systems using collaborative filtering, deep learning, and transformer-based models
- Conduct A/B experiments to measure model impact on key business metrics (CTR, conversion, revenue)
- Develop and maintain feature engineering pipelines processing 10TB+ daily event data
- Perform exploratory data analysis to identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities
- Optimize model inference latency for real-time serving at scale
- Mentor junior data scientists and contribute to team best practices
- Present findings and recommendations to senior leadership

Requirements:
- MS or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Machine Learning, or related field
- 5+ years of experience in applied machine learning or data science
- Strong proficiency in Python, SQL, and ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn)
- Experience with large-scale data processing (Spark, Hadoop, or equivalent)
- Hands-on experience with recommendation systems or NLP
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS SageMaker, EMR, S3, Redshift)
- Experience with experiment design and statistical analysis (hypothesis testing, causal inference)
- Strong communication skills with ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders

Nice to Have:
- Experience with LLMs and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
- Knowledge of MLOps tools (MLflow, Kubeflow, Airflow)
- Publications in top ML/AI conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, KDD)
- Experience with real-time serving infrastructure (Kafka, Flink, Redis)

Compensation: $180,000 – $240,000 base + equity + benefits
